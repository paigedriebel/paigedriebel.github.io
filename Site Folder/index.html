The Back Room

We truly value the vibrancy and artistic energy that music brings to our cafes and community, which meant a partnership with the Pabst Theater Group was a natural fit. Our former roastery now serves as an intimate stop for more than 150 small-scale touring and local artists each year. Adorned with string lights, hanging plants, and the smell of freshly brewed coffee, The Back Room @ Colectivo provides a perfect space for an up-close-and-personal evening with any artist, from jazz musicians to the next big thing in indie.

Andy Shauf

July 11th, 2020

Few artists are storytellers as deft and disarmingly observational as Andy Shauf. The Toronto-based, Saskatchewan-raised musician’s songs unfold like short fiction: they’re densely layered with colorful characters and a rich emotional depth. On his new album The Neon Skyline (out now via ANTI-), he sets a familiar scene of inviting a friend for beers on the opening title track: “I said, ‘Come to the Skyline, I’ll be washing my sins away.’ He just laughed, said ‘I’ll be late, you know how I can be.”
